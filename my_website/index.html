Meet Timber, the cutest pup of all time

Learn more about my parents' dog

This is Timber. He is a golden retreiver and is about 1.5 years old. His nickname is Tim. My parents got him a little over a year ago. Since I don't have a dog of my own, I love visiting my parents back in Oregon so that I can play with their puppy!

Timber is very friendly and loving. When you start petting him, he "melts" to the floor and rolls on his back for a belly rub almost every time. He is a good adventure pup and we take him on hikes with us often. He is also very mellow for a puppy, which has been nice for my parents, especially when training him.

Here are some facts about golden retreivers:

  • Golden Retrievers consistently rank among the top 3 most popular dogs in the U.S.
  • They have a long life span of 12-14 years.
  • They are natural athletes and love to swim.
  • They're easy to train, making them great therapy and service dogs, as well as search and rescue dogs.
